Welcome to the Database



Here is a quick overview of some things you can do.

  • Look up League of Legends characters in the "Select a Character" tab.
  • Find specific info regarding that character (lore, abilities, faction, etc.)
  • You can also add your own custom character to the database under the "Custom Characters" tab
  • In order to create your own custom characters, you first need to log-in.
  • The characters you create will be accessible to you whenever you log-in
  • While logged in, you can also delete your custom made characters(but not the characters in the select tab)
